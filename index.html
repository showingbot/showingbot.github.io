How it Works

1

Buyer's agent texts the ShowingBot number with a showing request. If it's their first time texting us, we'll confirm their agent credentials. Then on every additional request we'll remember the agent associated with the phone number.

2

ShowingBot automatically generates a text or email to the sellers (based on their preference). The sellers only need to respond with Yes or No to accept or decline the showing.

3

Once confirmed by the sellers, another text is automatically sent to the buyer's agent to notify them, along with any other showing instructions.
